About Us

A family in Duck Run 2.

We've grown slowly — at the speed of the Holy Spirit and friendship. This is our story so far.

Who We Are

We're a Pentecostal Christian congregation in Duck Run 2, a village of around 455 residents in Cayo District, just west of Spanish Lookout.

The church is small but active: families of every age — children, youth, and grandparents — who gather twice a week to worship, study the Bible, and pray. A children's Bible school is currently under construction on the church grounds.

Our Denomination

We belong to Full Gospel Church of God Belize (FGCOG), a Pentecostal denomination with a national presence — Belmopan, Santa Elena, Orange Walk, San Pedro, San José, Santa Familia, and more.

With the national body we share a clear emphasis: the centrality of Christ, the authority of the Bible, the work of the Holy Spirit, and evangelism as a daily mission.

What We Believe

We believe the full gospel — salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, water baptism, the baptism of the Holy Spirit, divine healing, and the soon return of the Lord.

We believe the local church exists for three things: to worship God, to make disciples, and to serve its community.

Doctrine

What we teach

1

The Bible

Inspired by God, sufficient, and our final authority in faith and life.

2

One God

Eternal — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. Three persons, one essence.

3

Jesus Christ

Fully God and fully man, virgin-born, crucified, risen, the only Savior.

4

Salvation

By grace, through faith in Christ — not by works, but for good works.

5

Holy Spirit

Lives in the believer, empowers witness, gives gifts to build the church.

6

Baptism

Water baptism by immersion as a public confession of faith in Jesus.

7

Divine Healing

God still heals today. We pray in faith and trust His will.

8

The Church

Christ's body on earth — local, visible, sent to make disciples.

9

Second Coming

Christ is coming again. We wait, and we live ready.
