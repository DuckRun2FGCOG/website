Our Place

A small church with a great God.

Full Gospel Church of God – Duck Run 2 is a Pentecostal Christian congregation in the heart of the Cayo district, Belize. We are part of Full Gospel Church of God. We are a group of united churches from Santa Marta, Armenia, Belmopan, Santa Elena, Contiki, Benque Viejo, Santa Familia, and Duck Run 2.

We are a church that believes in the move and power of the Holy Spirit to heal, restore, and save.
