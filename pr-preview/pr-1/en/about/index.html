About Us

The Full Gospel Church of God at Duck Run 2 is part of the Full Gospel Church of God Belize (FGCOG) denomination.

Denomination

FGCOG is a Pentecostal denomination with a significant presence across Belize, with congregations in Belmopan, Santa Elena, Orange Walk, San Pedro, San Jose, Santa Familia, and other communities.
