Your first visit

Come as you are.

Here's what you'll find the first time you walk through the door.

01

Hello at the door

Someone will hold the door and say good morning. Sit anywhere you like — the front rows are always open.

02

Worship

We sing together, loud. Guitar, keyboard, drums, hands raised. Join in or just listen — no pressure.

03

Preaching

Someone opens the Bible and works through a passage. It might be Pastor Ismael or one of the church brothers — we take turns. Bring a notebook if it helps; many do.

04

Prayer and farewell

We close in prayer. If you want personal prayer, leaders stay after. Coffee outside — and on Wednesdays, a shared meal.

Common questions

Is there a dress code?

No. People come however they can — jeans, dress, work pants. Showing up is what matters.

What language is the service in?

Spanish, mostly. If you bring someone who only speaks English, we'll welcome them and help them follow along.

Is there anything for the kids?

Children stay in the service and there are moments just for them. A children's Bible school is currently being built on the church grounds — soon we'll have age-based teaching too.

How long does it last?

Around an hour and a half. Come on time if you can, but if you're late, come anyway.
